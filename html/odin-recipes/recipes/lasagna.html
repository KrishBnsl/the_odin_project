Making Lasagna



Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Easy Lasagna Ingredients

these are the ingredients you'll need to make this lasagna easily:
  • Beef:

    This easy lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use ground turkey for a lighter option

  • Spaggheti Sauce:

    Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti sauce

  • Cheese:

    You’ll need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
